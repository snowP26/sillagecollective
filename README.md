# Sillage Collective
**Sillage Collective** is a fragrance-centered social platform where enthusiasts can share their thoughts, curate collections, build wishlists, and discover new scents through an AI-powered recommendation system.

Inspired by the idea of *sillage*—the scent trail you leave behind—the platform focuses on personal expression, community discovery, and intelligent fragrance matching.

---
## ■ Core Concept
Sillage Collective allows users to:
- Share fragrance reviews and personal scent stories
- Showcase their fragrance collections
- Maintain wishlists for future purchases
- Discover new fragrances through AI-powered recommendations
- Engage with a growing community of fragrance enthusiasts
